About Zaelet Precision Company

Zaelet Precision Company is a leading global manufacturer of high-precision geospatial instruments for engineering professionals. With our headquarters in Shanghai, China and an active branch in Accra, Ghana, we are strategically positioned to serve customers across Asia, Africa, and beyond.

With a focus on innovation and quality, we provide cutting-edge solutions for surveying, mapping, and geospatial data collection to clients worldwide. Our international team of engineers and specialists ensures that our products meet the diverse needs of professionals across different regions and environments.

Our comprehensive range of products includes GNSS receivers, total stations, theodolites, levels, and specialized software solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of our global customer base. Through our expanding network of distributors and partners, we're committed to bringing precision technology to every corner of the world.
