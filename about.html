About Zaelet Precision

Our Story

Pioneering precision engineering since 2010

Zaelet Precision was founded in 2010 with a clear mission: to make high-precision geospatial technology accessible to professionals worldwide. What began as a small team of engineers in Shanghai has grown into a global company with a presence across multiple continents.

Our journey has been driven by innovation, quality, and a deep understanding of our customers' needs. By combining cutting-edge technology with practical design, we've developed a comprehensive range of surveying and mapping solutions that deliver exceptional performance at competitive prices.

Today, Zaelet is recognized as a leading provider of geospatial instruments, with a particular focus on emerging markets and developing economies. Our headquarters in Shanghai, China, and our branch office in Accra, Ghana, allow us to serve customers globally while maintaining a strong presence in key regions.

Our Mission

To democratize access to high-precision geospatial technology by providing innovative, reliable, and affordable solutions that empower professionals to work more efficiently and accurately.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in accessible geospatial technology, driving innovation that connects the physical and digital worlds through precision measurement and data collection.

Our Values

Innovation, Quality, Accessibility, Customer Focus, and Global Perspective guide everything we do at Zaelet Precision.
