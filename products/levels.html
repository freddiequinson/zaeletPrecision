RAL4 Automatic Level

High-Precision Automatic Level

The RAL4 is a high-precision automatic level designed for professional surveyors and engineers who require accurate elevation measurements. With its 1.5mm accuracy per 1km of double leveling and 32X magnification, the RAL4 delivers exceptional performance for construction layout, topographic surveys, and other precision applications.

The RAL4 features a magnetic-damped compensator that automatically levels the line of sight, ensuring accurate readings even in challenging conditions. Its robust construction and water-resistant design make it ideal for use in various environments.

Technical Specifications

Accuracy

  • 1.5mm per 1km double leveling

Telescope

  • Magnification: 32X
  • Field of view: 1°20'
  • Minimum focus: 0.3m

Compensator

  • Type: Magnetic-damped
  • Setting accuracy: ±0.3"
  • Working range: ±15'

Physical

  • Weight: 1.8kg
  • Dimensions: 200 x 135 x 130mm
  • Water resistance: IP54

  • High-precision 1.5mm accuracy per 1km of double leveling
