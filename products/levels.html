Digital & Auto Levels

Zaelet's digital and automatic levels provide precise height measurements for various surveying and construction applications.

ZDL-32 Digital Level

High-Precision Digital Level

The ZDL-32 digital level is designed for high-precision leveling tasks in construction and surveying projects. With its advanced digital technology and user-friendly interface, it provides fast and accurate measurements.

Featuring automatic height and distance measurements, internal memory for data storage, and USB data transfer capability, the ZDL-32 streamlines your leveling workflows.

Key Features

  • 0.7mm/km double-run leveling accuracy
  • 2-second electronic measurement
  • Internal memory for 2,000 measurements
  • USB data transfer
  • Large LCD display with illumination
  • 20-hour battery life

ZAL-32 Auto Level

Professional Automatic Level

The ZAL-32 is a professional automatic level that combines reliability with precision. Its magnetic-dampened compensator ensures quick and stable readings, while the water-resistant design makes it suitable for all weather conditions.

Perfect for construction sites, civil engineering projects, and general surveying tasks, the ZAL-32 delivers consistent accuracy and durability at an affordable price point.

Key Features

  • 32x magnification
  • Magnetic-dampened compensator
  • ±15' compensator range
  • Water-resistant construction
  • 1.5mm/km leveling accuracy
  • Endless horizontal drive

ZAL-24 Auto Level

Entry-Level Automatic Level

The ZAL-24 is an entry-level automatic level that offers excellent value for basic surveying and construction tasks. With its 24x magnification and robust design, it's perfect for general construction, landscaping, and basic surveying applications.

Built with durability in mind, the ZAL-24 features a shock-resistant housing and simple operation, making it ideal for both professionals and beginners.

Key Features

  • 24x magnification
  • Air-dampened compensator
  • ±15' compensator range
  • Shock-resistant housing
  • 2.0mm/km leveling accuracy
