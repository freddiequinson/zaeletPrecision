Disteo23

High-Precision Electronic Theodolite

The Disteo23 is a high-precision electronic theodolite designed for professional surveyors who require accurate angle measurements and distance calculations. With its 2" angle accuracy and 300m distance range with prism, the Disteo23 is perfect for construction layout, boundary surveys, and other precision applications.

Featuring an alphanumeric keyboard and built-in stake out program, the Disteo23 combines traditional theodolite functionality with modern electronic features for enhanced productivity in the field.

Technical Specifications

Angle Measurement

  • Angle Accuracy: 2"

Distance Measurement

  • Distance Range (with prism): 300m
  • Distance Accuracy: 3mm+2ppm
