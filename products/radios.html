Radio Communication Systems

Zaelet's radio communication systems provide reliable data transmission for GNSS/RTK surveying and precise positioning applications.

UHF eRadio HX-DU8616D

Smart and High Efficient Radio Modem for Fast Data Transmission

HX-DU8616D is an advanced, high speed, high power, wireless data link built to withstand the demanding conditions of GNSS/RTK surveying and precise positioning. Due to the 35W high transmitting power, connection distances up to 20 kilometers can be covered in favorable conditions. It is designed with a ruggedized IP67 weatherproof enclosure to withstand extreme environments.

Technical Specifications

General

  • Frequency: 410MHz~470MHz
  • Operating Mode: Transceiver, Transmitter, Radio Repeater
  • Channel Width: 12.5KHz/25KHz
  • Channels: Up to 32 programmable channels (user selectable)
  • Operating Voltage: 9V~16V
  • Frequency Stability: <±1ppm
  • Ingress Protection Rating: IP67

Power Consumption

  • Transmit Output Power H:(35W) 85W@12V DC
  • Transmit Output Power M:(22W) 60W@12V DC
  • Transmit Output Power L:(5W) 35W@12V DC
  • Sleep State: 2W@12V DC

Structural

  • Dimension: 175(H)×130(W)×86.5(D)mm
  • Weight: 2kg
  • Operating Temperature: -40℃ to +65℃
  • Storage Temperature: -50℃ to +85℃
  • Connector: TNC female
  • Data Connector: LEMO 5pin

Transmitter & Receiver

  • RF Output Power H: 35W 45.4±0.5dBm @DC 12V
  • RF Output Power M: 22W 43.4±0.5dBm @DC 12V
  • RF Output Power L: 5W 37±1dBm @DC12V
  • RF Power Stability: ±1dB
  • Sensitivity: <-114dBm@BER 10-5，9600bps

Data Modem & Bluetooth

  • Data Rate: 4800bps/9600bps/19200bps
  • Data Speed of Serial Interface: 9600、19200、38400、57600、115200bps
  • Modulation: GMSK/4FSK
  • Bluetooth Version: 2.0/4.0
  • Bluetooth Antenna: Built-in

Key Features

  • High transmitting power up to 35W for long-distance communication
  • IP67 weatherproof enclosure for extreme environments
  • Multiple operating modes: Transceiver, Transmitter, Radio Repeater
  • Built-in Bluetooth for wireless connectivity
  • Wide operating temperature range from -40℃ to +65℃
  • Up to 32 programmable channels for flexible operation
