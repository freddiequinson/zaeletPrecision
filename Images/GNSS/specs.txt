Zaelet F1 Mini
Zaelet F1 Mini is a mini portable multi-function GNSS receiver with a new generation of measurement engine, support for tilt measurement, built-in 4G full network access and Bluetooth/WIFI, a new appearance design, magnesium alloy structure, Linux operating system, and is an ultimate, multi-function, lightweight and portable geodetic GNSS receiver.
 Linux+Qualcomm MDM9628 Cortex-A7 intelligent system platform brings users efficient computing and unlimited expansion of product functions.
● The host integrates a high-precision positioning module, supports BDS B1/B2/B3, GPS L1/L2/L5, GLONASS L1/L2, Galileo E1/E5a/E5b, SBAS L1, QZSS L1/L2/L5, and full-system and full-frequency signal reception and resolution.
● Built-in intelligent high-precision inertial navigation module and real-time tilt compensation eliminate the “flying spot” in RTK measurement.
● The 4G full network solution based on the Linux platform fully supports China Mobile/China Unicom/China Telecom 2/3/4G networks, with better compatibility, stronger signals and more stable connections.
● New four-in-one antenna, integrating GNSS, WiFi, Bluetooth and 4G, with smaller size and better signal.
