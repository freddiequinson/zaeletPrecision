Zaelet T3 Electronic Theodolite

Precision Angle Measurement Instrument

Product Overview

The Zaelet T3 Electronic Theodolite is a high-precision angle measurement instrument designed for surveying, construction, and engineering applications. With its advanced electronic display and accurate measurement capabilities, it delivers exceptional performance in all environments.

Key Features

  • High-precision 2" angle accuracy
  • Large LCD display with backlight for all lighting conditions
  • Dual-axis compensator for automatic level correction
  • Rechargeable battery with 20+ hours of continuous operation
  • Optical plummet for easy and accurate setup
  • Convenient alphanumeric keyboard for data entry
  • Durable construction for reliable performance in various environments
