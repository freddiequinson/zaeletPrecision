Zaelet L4 Digital Level

High-Precision Digital Leveling System

Product Overview

The Zaelet L4 Digital Level is a high-precision digital leveling system designed for surveying, construction, and engineering applications. With its advanced electronic measurement capabilities and user-friendly interface, it delivers exceptional accuracy and efficiency in all environments.

Key Features

  • High-precision digital measurement with 0.3mm accuracy
  • Large LCD display with backlight for all lighting conditions
  • Electronic reading of height and distance
  • Internal memory for storing up to 2,000 measurements
  • USB port for data transfer to computer
  • Rechargeable battery with 20+ hours of operation
  • Compact and lightweight design for easy transport
  • Durable construction for reliable field performance

Applications

  • Construction layout and monitoring
  • Topographic surveying
  • Elevation control for infrastructure projects
  • Foundation setting and verification
  • Precise leveling for engineering applications
