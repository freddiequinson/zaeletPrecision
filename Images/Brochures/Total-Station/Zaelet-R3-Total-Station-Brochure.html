Zaelet R3 Total Station

Premium Robotic Total Station

Product Overview

The Zaelet R3 Total Station is our premium robotic total station designed for the most demanding surveying, engineering, and construction applications. With its advanced features and rugged design, it delivers exceptional performance in all environments.

Key Features

  • Extended non-prism range up to 1500m
  • Precise 2" angle accuracy
  • Dual-axis compensation for accurate leveling
  • Atmospheric sensor for environmental corrections
  • USB 2.0 port for fast data transfer
  • Compatible with Android applications
