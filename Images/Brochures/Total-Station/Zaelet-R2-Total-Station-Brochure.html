Zaelet R2 Total Station

Advanced Robotic Total Station

Product Overview

The Zaelet R2 Total Station is an advanced robotic total station designed for high-precision surveying and construction applications. With its Android operating system and touchscreen interface, it delivers exceptional performance and ease of use in all environments.

Key Features

  • Android 11 operating system for seamless app integration
  • High-resolution 5.5" color touchscreen
  • Precise 2" angle accuracy
  • Long-range non-prism measurement up to 1000m
  • Cellular connectivity for real-time data transfer
  • Bluetooth for wireless connections to external devices
