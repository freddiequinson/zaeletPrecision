Zaelet F1 Pro

High-Precision GNSS Receiver

Product Overview

The Zaelet F1 Pro is a high-precision GNSS receiver designed for professional surveying and mapping applications. With its advanced multi-constellation tracking capabilities and rugged design, it delivers exceptional performance in challenging environments.

Key Features

  • Multi-constellation tracking: GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, BeiDou, QZSS
  • High-precision RTK positioning with centimeter-level accuracy
  • Built-in 4G modem for network connectivity
  • Bluetooth and Wi-Fi connectivity
  • IP67 waterproof and dustproof rating
  • 8-hour battery life for all-day operation
  • Multiple work modes: Network RTK, Static & PPK
