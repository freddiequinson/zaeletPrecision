Zaelet R2 GNSS Receiver

Advanced RTK GNSS System

Product Overview

The Zaelet R2 GNSS Receiver is an advanced RTK GNSS system designed for high-precision surveying, mapping, and construction applications. With its state-of-the-art technology and rugged design, it delivers exceptional performance in challenging environments.

Key Features

  • Advanced multi-constellation tracking: GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, BeiDou, QZSS
  • High-precision RTK positioning with 8mm + 1ppm horizontal accuracy
  • Rugged design with IP68 waterproof and dustproof rating
  • Built-in UHF radio with extended range
  • Integrated 4G modem for network RTK
  • Voice guidance system for operational status
  • Anti-theft and remote management capabilities
