Zaelet R3 GNSS Receiver

Premium RTK GNSS System

Product Overview

The Zaelet R3 GNSS Receiver is our premium RTK GNSS system designed for the most demanding surveying, mapping, and construction applications. With its cutting-edge technology and ultra-rugged design, it delivers unmatched performance in all environments.

Key Features

  • Advanced multi-constellation tracking with 1000+ channels
  • High-precision RTK positioning with 5mm + 0.5ppm horizontal accuracy
  • Ultra-rugged design with IP68+ waterproof and dustproof rating
  • Built-in UHF radio with extended range up to 8km
  • Integrated 5G modem for high-speed network RTK
  • Advanced anti-jamming technology
  • Tilt compensation for pole-free operation
  • Voice guidance system in multiple languages
