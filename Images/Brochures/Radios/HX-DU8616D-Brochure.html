UHF eRadio HX-DU8616D

Smart and High Efficient Radio Modem for Fast Data Transmission

Product Overview

HX-DU8616D is an advanced, high speed, high power, wireless data link built to withstand the demanding conditions of GNSS/RTK surveying and precise positioning. Due to the 35W high transmitting power, connection distances up to 20 kilometers can be covered in favorable conditions. It is designed with a ruggedized IP67 weatherproof enclosure to withstand extreme environments.

The HX-DU8616D features excellent performance with full GNSS technology, offers best-in-class signal tracking and performs data surveys beyond the usual limitations. With multiple operating modes and programmable channels, it provides maximum flexibility for various surveying applications.

Key Features

High Power Transmission

Up to 35W transmitting power for long-distance communication up to 20 kilometers in favorable conditions.

Ruggedized Design

IP67 weatherproof enclosure designed to withstand extreme environments and harsh field conditions.

Multiple Operating Modes

Functions as a Transceiver, Transmitter, or Radio Repeater for maximum versatility in the field.

Wireless Connectivity

Built-in Bluetooth 2.0/4.0 for easy configuration and seamless integration with other devices.

Extreme Temperature Range

Wide operating temperature range from -40℃ to +65℃ ensures reliability in any climate.

Programmable Channels

Up to 32 programmable channels for flexible operation in various environments.
